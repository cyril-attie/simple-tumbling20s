The Tumbling 20's

Vision

The Tumbling20's fund is a safe haven to place savings during the Tumbling 20's. Also we want to be a first-point-of-contact and promoter of Bitcoin and Ethereum communities that stand up for fundamental rights. In particular we value security, equity and stabilibity in the plans put forward by these communities.

Mission

Our mission is to catalyze monetary change. That includes to prepare people to soothe the Crisis distress, to promote aforementioned public blockchains in order to put an end to banking domination and to provide information and investment services regarding the Tumbling 20's and Blockchain take over.

Strategy

To take the lid off banking domination of politics through finance. Soon we expect run-on-banks, demonstrations and tumbling financial markets to promote Bitcoin and Ethereum. We target all the people, from 7-year-old kids discovering the world to 70-year-old baby boomers aiming to secure their savings during the Tumbling 20's.
