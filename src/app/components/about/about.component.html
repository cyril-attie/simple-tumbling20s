The Tumbling 20's

Vision

Our vision is to become a first point of contact for the blockchain community that is implicitly standing up for fundamental rights. In particular we acknowledge noble ideals like security, equity and stabilibity in the plans put forward by blockchain communities like Bitcoin and Ethereum.

Mission

Our mission includes to prepare people to soothe the Crisis distress, to promote aforementioned public blockchains to put an end to banking-provoked crises and feudal-banking system and to provide information and investment services regarding the Tumbling 20's and Blockchain take over.

Strategy

We wrote this website to take the lid off banking domination of politics through finance. Soon we expect run on banks, demonstrations and tumbling financial markets to do our marketing. We target all the people, 7 years old kids discovering the world to baby boomers secure and multiply their savings.
